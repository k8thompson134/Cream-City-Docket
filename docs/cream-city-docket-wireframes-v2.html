Plain-Language Summary
This ordinance requires landlords in Milwaukee to give tenants 90 days notice before raising rent by more than 5%. It also creates a new dispute resolution process through the city clerk's office where tenants can file complaints about improper notice. Violations would result in fines for landlords. If passed, the ordinance would take effect the day after publication in the city newspaper.
